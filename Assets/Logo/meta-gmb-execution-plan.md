# Preet Tech – Meta Ads & GMB Execution Plan

**Offer:** Eco Website under ₹3,999  
**Goal:** ₹1,00,000 revenue in 30 days  
**Primary Channels:** Meta Ads (Facebook + Instagram), Google Business Profile (GMB)  

---

## 1. Objectives & KPIs

**Business Objectives (30 days)**
- Sell minimum 25–30 eco websites at ₹3,999 (target ≥ ₹1,00,000 revenue).
- Build a qualified local lead database of 80–120 business owners in Uttarakhand.
- Improve brand visibility for Preet Tech in Dehradun, Haridwar, Haldwani, Rishikesh, Rudrapur, Kashipur, Roorkee, and nearby hubs.

**Primary KPIs**
- Cost per Lead (CPL) from Meta Ads.
- Number of qualified inbound calls/messages from GMB.
- Lead-to-sale conversion rate.
- Total revenue from eco website offer.

---

## 2. Core Offer & Funnel

**Offer Structure**
- Product: Eco Website under ₹3,999 (mobile responsive, fast loading, SEO-friendly basic structure).
- Target Segments: Beauty & Wellness, Hospitality & Travel, Fitness & Lifestyle, Education, local SMEs and professionals.

**Marketing Funnel**
1. Awareness: Meta Ads (Reels, image ads, story ads) + GMB visibility in local search.
2. Interest: Landing page / lead form explaining eco website benefits + industry-specific examples.
3. Consideration: WhatsApp/phone consultation and demo of sample templates.
4. Conversion: Close sale at ₹3,999 and collect basic content for website.
5. Upsell: After delivery, pitch social media handling, performance marketing, and additional tools.

---

## 3. Meta Ads Execution Plan

### 3.1 Campaign Structure

**Campaign 1 – Lead Generation: Eco Website Offer**
- Objective: Lead Generation (Instant Forms) or Leads via WhatsApp.
- Budget: 70–80% of total Meta budget.
- Optimization: Leads (higher intent), not traffic.

**Ad Sets (within Campaign 1)**
- Ad Set A: Broad Uttarakhand – Age 25–55, interests in digital marketing, online business, website building, social media marketing.
- Ad Set B: Industry Focus – Beauty & Wellness, Fitness, Education, Hospitality interests + business owners.
- Ad Set C: Retargeting – Website visitors, page engagers, past leads (if available).

**Campaign 2 – Retargeting / Nurturing (Optional in Week 3–4)**
- Objective: Messages or Leads.
- Target: People who opened lead form, visited website, or engaged with ads but did not convert.

### 3.2 Targeting Details

**Location Targeting**
- Primary Cities: Dehradun, Haridwar, Roorkee, Rishikesh, Haldwani, Nainital, Rudrapur, Kashipur, Kichha, Gadarpur.
- Radius: 10–25 km around city centres depending on population density.

**Demographics**
- Age: 25–55.
- Gender: All.
- Profile: Business owners, self-employed, managers, startup founders, local entrepreneurs.

**Interests**
- Core: Digital marketing, Social media marketing, Website building, Online business, Entrepreneurship, Technology tools, Small business.
- Industry-specific overlays:
  - Salons, beauty parlours, spas, boutiques.
  - Gyms, yoga studios, fitness trainers, Zumba.
  - Schools, colleges, coaching centres.
  - Hotels, homestays, travel agencies, restaurants.

### 3.3 Creatives & Ad Copy

**Formats**
- 1–2 Short vertical videos (10–20 seconds) showing before/after website screens.
- 2–3 static images highlighting price (₹3,999) and benefits.
- 1 carousel with industry-specific layouts (Salon, Gym, Coaching, Hotel).

**Messaging Guidelines**
- Hook: “Apne business ka professional website sirf ₹3,999 mein – Uttarakhand ke local businesses ke liye.”
- Benefits: Mobile-friendly, fast, WhatsApp integration, enquiry form, Google-ready.
- Urgency: Limited slots, monthly cap on new websites for quality.
- CTA: “Fill the form for free demo”, “WhatsApp for details”, “Book a call today”.

### 3.4 Lead Forms

**Lead Form Fields**
- Full Name.
- Mobile Number (WhatsApp preferred).
- Business Name.
- City / Area.
- Business Type (Salon, Gym, Coaching, Hotel, Restaurant, Travel Agency, Other).
- Question: “Do you already have a website?” (Yes / No).

**Thank You Screen**
- Message: “Thank you! Our team will contact you in few hours with a custom eco website demo.”
- Optional: Button to WhatsApp chat or landing page for more details.

### 3.5 Budget & Timeline (Meta Ads)

Assuming total budget: ₹10,000–₹15,000 for 30 days.

**Week 1 – Testing (₹3,000–₹4,000)**
- Run Ad Set A and Ad Set B with 2–3 creatives each.
- Split budget evenly between A and B.
- Objective: Identify creatives and audiences with best CPL and lead quality.

**Week 2 – Optimization (₹3,000–₹4,000)**
- Pause worst-performing creatives/ad sets.
- Allocate 70% budget to the best ad set (A or B) and 30% to the second-best.
- Start building a retargeting audience (engagers and form opens).

**Week 3 – Scaling + Retargeting (₹2,000–₹4,000)**
- Increase daily budget on winning ad set by 20–30% if CPL is acceptable.
- Launch Campaign 2 (Retargeting) with small budget for warm leads.

**Week 4 – Final Push (₹2,000–₹3,000)**
- Focus budget on:
  - Best-performing prospecting ad set.
  - Retargeting ad set with strong CTA and offer reminder.
- Offer small bonus: free 1-month basic support or minor update to close hesitant leads.

### 3.6 Daily & Weekly Tasks (Meta Ads)

**Daily (30–45 minutes)**
- Check spend and CPL for each ad set.
- Respond to all leads via WhatsApp/call within 1–2 hours.
- Mark lead status in a simple Google Sheet or CRM (New, Contacted, Demo Scheduled, Closed, Not Interested).

**Weekly (60–90 minutes)**
- Review performance by ad set, creative, and city.
- Turn off ads with high CPL or poor lead quality.
- Duplicate best performer and test a new creative or copy variation.
- Adjust budget based on best-performing segments.

## 4. Google Business Profile (GMB) Execution Plan

### 4.1 Setup & Optimization

**Step 1 – Create/Claim Profile**
- Search “Preet Tech” on Google and claim existing profile or create a new one.
- Ensure correct business name: “Preet Tech – Web Design & Digital Marketing”.

**Step 2 – Business Info**
- Category: Website designer / Digital marketing agency.
- Service Areas: Dehradun, Haldwani, Haridwar, Rishikesh, Rudrapur, Kashipur, Roorkee, nearby towns.
- Business Hours: Include realistic working hours with after-hours WhatsApp availability if applicable.
- Phone Number: Primary sales/WhatsApp number.
- Website: Link to eco website landing page.

**Step 3 – Services & Description**
- Add services: Eco Website under ₹3,999, Web Design, Social Media Management, Performance Marketing, App Development, Business Tools.
- Description highlights:
  - Eco website at ₹3,999 for local businesses.
  - Industries you serve (Salon, Gym, Coaching, Hotel, etc.).
  - Local presence in Uttarakhand.

### 4.2 Visuals & Content

**Photos & Videos**
- Logo and brand cover image.
- Office/working photos (even if home office, keep it professional).
- Screenshots/mockups of sample websites for salons, gyms, coaching centres, hotels.

**Google Posts (2–3 per week)**
- Post Type: Offer / What’s New.
- Content ideas:
  - “New Offer: Get your professional website for just ₹3,999 – Ideal for salons, gyms, coaching centres and hotels.”
  - “Uttarakhand businesses now get fast-loading eco websites with WhatsApp integration.”
  - “Limited slots this month – book your eco website today.”
- Each post should include: short copy, image, CTA button (Call, Learn More, Sign Up) linking to WhatsApp or landing page.

### 4.3 Reviews & Social Proof

**Collecting Reviews**
- After each successful website project, ask the client for a Google review.
- Provide them a prepared link and sample review structure (service name + location + result).

**Using Reviews in Marketing**
- Highlight best reviews in Meta Ads creatives.
- Add review screenshots to website and GMB photos.

### 4.4 Lead Handling from GMB

**Lead Sources**
- Direct phone calls from GMB.
- Website visits from GMB.
- Messages (if enabled).

**Process**
- Treat GMB leads as high-intent (they are already searching for a service).
- Respond quickly with a short script:
  - Understand business type and requirement.
  - Pitch eco website at ₹3,999 with benefits.
  - Offer demo or sample relevant to their industry.
- Log GMB leads separately in your lead sheet to track conversions.

## 5. Combined Funnel & Lead Management

### 5.1 Lead Capture System

Use a single Google Sheet or simple CRM with the following columns:
- Lead Source (Meta – Form, Meta – WhatsApp, GMB – Call, GMB – Website, Referral).
- Name.
- Phone / WhatsApp.
- City.
- Business Type.
- Current Website (Yes/No).
- Status (New, Contacted, Demo Scheduled, Closed Won, Closed Lost).
- Notes (budget, objections, next steps).

### 5.2 Follow-up Workflow

**Day 0 (same day as lead)**
- Call/WhatsApp within 1–2 hours.
- Send short intro + 1–2 relevant website samples.

**Day 1–3**
- If no response, send follow-up WhatsApp or SMS.
- Offer a specific time slot for a quick call.

**Day 4–7**
- Final reminder message with limited-time offer.

**Post-Sale**
- Once website is delivered, ask for:
  - GMB review.
  - Permission to use website as portfolio.
  - Opportunity to discuss social media / performance marketing packages.

## 6. Weekly Calendar (Execution Overview)

### Week 1
- Finalize eco website landing page and lead form.
- Set up and launch Meta campaign with 2–3 creatives and 2 ad sets.
- Create/optimize GMB profile and upload initial photos.
- Publish first 2–3 Google Posts promoting the offer.

### Week 2
- Analyze Meta performance; pause low-performing creatives.
- Scale best-performing ad set moderately.
- Continue GMB posts (2 per week).
- Start collecting and showcasing first reviews (if any websites sold).

### Week 3
- Launch retargeting ads on Meta (for engagers and form opens).
- Test a new creative angle (e.g., targeted to a single industry like salons or gyms).
- Push a strong offer post on GMB.

### Week 4
- Focus Meta budget on proven best audiences and creatives.
- Promote a “Last week of offer” angle.
- Boost reviews and showcase success stories from earlier weeks.
